------------------README_678MHz.txt--------------------

Proof of concept LTSPice simulation of 6.78MHz wireless charger emitter
using GaNPower GaN+silicon driver copackage (GPI65030CO). 

Topology and design method based on ref:
"Single-Stage 6.78 MHz Power-Amplifier Design
Using High-Voltage GaN Power ICs for Wireless
Charging Applications," Lingxiao Xue, Jason Zhang,
March 2017,  DOI: 10.1109/APEC.2017.7931237,
2017 IEEE Applied Power Electronics Conference and Exposition (APEC)
